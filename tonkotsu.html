Tonkotsu Broth

Traditional Pork Bone Broth

Ingredients

  • 4 lbs. of Pork bones
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 bulb of garlic
  • 2 inch piece of ginger
  • 2 leeks
  • 2 tbsp. of sesame oil
  • Shoyu tare (to taste)
  • water

Steps

  1. Clean the Pork Bones
    1. Rinse and clean the Pork bones
    2. Blanch the Pork bones
    3. Place the Pork bones in a large pot and cover them in water
    4. Bring to boil and let boil for 12 minutes
  2. Prepare the Broth
    1. In a large stockpot, add the Pork Bones and water to cover
    2. Bring to boil on high heat and reduce to rolling boil
    3. Add the onion, garlic, ginger, and leeks whole to the pot
  3. Simmer the Broth
    1. Simmer the pot for 12-18 hours adding water as needed to maintain water covering the bones
    2. Skim any impurities with a strainer every couple of hours
  4. Strain the Broth
    1. Strain the broth through a fine mesh sieve or cheesecloth into a clean pot
    2. Add sesame oil
  5. Season the Broth
    1. Add the Shoyu Tare to taste (roughly 1-2 tbsp. per serving made)
